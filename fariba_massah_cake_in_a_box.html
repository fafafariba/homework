Description

What is cake in a box you say? Cake in a box is cake that comes prepackaged in a box at a fraction of the cost of fancy store or restaurant cake plus ten times more yummy.*

*This statement has been scientifically verified. Don't believe me? Check out wikipedia

Ingredients

  • Cake Mix from Box of Choice
  • 1 c Milk (or More)
  • 3 Any Sized Eggs (because I hate it when they specify egg size, like it really makes that much of a difference)
  • 3/4 c Oil

Directions

  1. Mix everything together in a mixing bowl that's lying around somewhere (preferably one that's clean)
  2. Mix some more
  3. And then some more
  4. Oil the baking pan of your choice (again not gonna tell you what size becaus I hate it when they tell you the dimensions of the pan)
  5. Oh yeah, preheat the oven. Shit. Now you have to wait for the oven to get hot.
  6. Ok, oven's hot, put it in the oven for like 25 minutes
  7. MOST IMPORTANT PART: Lick the mixing bowl clean
  8. After 25 minutes, make sure cake is not burnt. Stick a fork in the cake. If there's any residue on the fork prongs, needs more baking. If not, then it's ready for your consumption and pleasure.
